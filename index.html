About me

Hello! I'm Rashmi Hegde, an engineer by day and a passionate drawing artist by night. Blending my love for art and engineering, I create intricate drawings that reflect my meticulous attention to detail and creative flair. From vibrant landscapes to detailed portraits, each piece tells a unique story. Welcome to my portfolio—I hope you find inspiration in my work!
